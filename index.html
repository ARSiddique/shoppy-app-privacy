Privacy Policy

App: Shopy App · Effective date: 2025-09-18

Shopy App helps you manage retail operations such as products, sales, and reporting. This policy describes what information we handle, why we handle it, and the choices you have.

Information we handle

Account information
Name, email or similar details you provide to sign in. Authentication is powered by Firebase.
Business content you create
Products, invoices, customers and related records that you add while using the app. This content is stored in Firebase Firestore under your account.
Media you choose
Images you explicitly pick for product photos, receipts or your profile picture. These files may be uploaded to Firebase Storage so that they sync across your devices.
Diagnostics
Basic logs and crash reports to keep the app reliable and secure.

How we use the information

Information is used to provide app features, to sync your data securely, and to improve stability and security. We do not use your data for advertising or sell your personal information.

Permissions

Read media images (android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES): Access occurs only when you choose a photo for product images, receipts or your profile picture. Shopy App reads only the files you select; there is no background scanning of your library.

Sharing and processors

We rely on trusted service providers—primarily Google Firebase—to store and process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality and security commitments.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Access is limited to your account and authorized personnel when required to operate the service.

Retention and deletion

Your content is kept while your account remains active. You may delete data inside the app. To request account removal or data deletion, contact us at abdulraufsiddique10@gmail.com.

Children

Shopy App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes

We may revise this policy as the app evolves. We will post updates on this page with a new effective date.

Contact
